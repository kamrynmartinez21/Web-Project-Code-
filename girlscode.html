Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science around the world. The organization is working to close the gender employment difference in technology and change the image of what a programmer looks like.

Girls Who Code was founded by Reshma Saujani and her mission is to build the largest pipeline of future female engineers in the United States.
